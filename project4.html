FIGURE POSTER

This project calls upon me to design a poster for a figure of my choice. As a designer, I naturally looked to the field of design for inspiration, and decidede that the figure of my choie would be Apple's Cheif Design Offcier -- Jony Ive, as I think his designs, both hardware, like the MacBook and the iMac and software, like the interface of macOS and iOS, have enabled and inspired designers across the spectrum to create their own amazing works.

As one of the enabled and inspired designers, I conceived the poster to showcase, juxtapose and integrate his works with him: First, I created silhouettes of his hardware designs to represent his vision of modern and minimalist design. Then, I placed the realistic photos of the actual designs symmertically to the silhouettes and a portrait of Jony Ive created using only vector tools in between the silhouettes and photo to accentuate his notion that hardware and software should be tightly integrated and the fact that Apple, under his lead, has done the best of that integration in the industry.
