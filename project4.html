XIAOCUN DAYOU

This project aims to build a platform that provides Chinese student in UC Davis with convient services like buying and selling used goods, searching for apartment roommates, ordering pick-ups and more. The platform is currently a collection WeChat applets, equivalent to web applets. The name is in Chinese and transliterates to “Small Village Big Goods.”

I was on the Product/UIUX design team. Currently, 70% percent of the Chinese students in UC Davis are using the product and it is still undergoing iteration as we speak.
