EVENT POSTER

For this project, I was taksed with designing a poster for an event of my choice. And I went back to my cultural familiarity to seek inspiration: the tea and Chinese culture. So I chose the Tea Expo at Shanghai as the event.

The composition of the poster is set on a old parchment-like background to accentuate the tone of traditional Chinese culture. And the bottom of the poster is an element representing the water surface of a tea cup with more tea dripping from the duct above, creating an Zen-like atmosphere of tea-tasting. Then various tea-related elements and placed onto the poster to further accentuate the theme.
